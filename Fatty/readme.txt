ROI locations for signal present (mammo-fatty-SP.tar.gz) and signal absent (mammo-fatty-SA.tar.gz) are available.  Filenames include roi_SP/SA_PhantomSeed.loc. Signal present locations has three columns (X, Y, lesion number) where X,Y are pixel locations in the mammogram, and lesion 0-3 represent calc, 4-7 mass.  Signal absent locations has five columns (X_vox, Y_vox, Z_vox, X_pix, Y_pix) where first three are corresponding phantom location in voxels, last two are pixel location within the mammogram.
